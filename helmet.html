Bike Helmet Design for Additive Manufacturing

What?

  • Develop a CAD model of a bike helmet with lattice structures suitable for additive manufacturing.
  • Conduct an FEM analysis to check whether the design is safe.
  • Build a prototype model using the FDM approach.

How?

  • Used solid and surface features on CATIA V5 to design the helmet.
  • Drop test was carried out through ANSYS to simulate the impact condition.
  • Printing model was simulated in the Simufact software.

Results

  • The cost and manufacturing time were comparatively decreased while not compromising the safety factor.
  • Lattice structures inside the part to reduce weight.
  • Design that cannot be manufactured with conventional methods.
  • Additional models of the helmet design.
  • Conventional models of current helmets were tried out to produce an optimal design.
